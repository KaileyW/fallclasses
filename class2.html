Developing Intelligent Content

This course explores the concepts and practices related to developing intelligent content in the field of technical communication. Students will learn how to create content that is adaptable, reusable, and able to be delivered across multiple platforms and formats. The course will cover the use of metadata, structured authoring, (component) content management systems and the application of artificial intelligence in content development.

Professor: Carlos Evia

Carlos Evia is the Associate Dean for Transdisciplinary Initiatives, Chief Technology Officer, and a professor in the School of Communication located in the College of Liberal Arts and Human Sciences at Virginia Tech. He is from Merida, Mexico, where he received his master's in computer systems before receiving his PhD in technical communication and rhetoric from Texas Technical University. His research interests are in content operations and strategies. Earlier this year he published a book titled, “Content Operations from Start to Scale” and has published 3 others in the past years. He enjoys eating steak, reading, listening to music, and watching movies.
